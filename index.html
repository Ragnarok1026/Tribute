Christopher Sabat

Christopher Robin Sabat is an American voice actor and dubbing director who is known for his English-language dubs of anime and video games. In addition to his voice acting, he is involved in directing and producing English adaptations of anime. His deep voice and versatility have made him a prominent figure in the voice acting community.

Characters voiced by Christopher Sabat

  • Vegita from Dragon Ball
  • Piccilo from Dragon Ball
  • Yamcha from Dragon Ball
  • Nappa from Dragon Ball
  • Zoro from One Piece
  • Yami Sukihiro From Black Clover
  • All Might from My Hero Acadamia
